A Bit About Myself!

I grew up around computers, and always enjoyed helping my father building them and maintaining them. However after graduating from high school I was a drift and didn't know what I wanted to do. I got a job doing shipping and receiving for a machine making company. After I was laid off, I got a job through a temp agency working for an HVAC company. Through this company I got the opportunity to start a career in CNC Machining with Mills and Lathes. The Head Machinist took me as his apprentice. By the end of my 4 years with that company I was the Head Machinist with a promising future, but Covid had other plans for me. Once unemployed I found it difficult to find a job in that field so I decided I would take my programming knowledge from machining and try to apply the concepts here at Epicodus. It is difficult and most certainly different, but it is good to work my brain again.
